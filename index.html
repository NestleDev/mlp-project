Overview

You own planets to grow various organics. Each planet is better adapted to a specific species, so you can own different planets and grow grass, trees or stones there.

Complete orders and exchanges resources with other players to receive in-game tokens that can be spent on equipment or other upgrades that increase in-game income.

You are the owner of the farm and the planet in general, personally extracting resources by special technologies and this is what differentiates the game from the other similar farming simulators and takes the players' attention.

We not only consider that Play-To-Earn games are fun to play, but we also believe that the gameplay is able to keep your interest for a long time.

We're making a metaverse of hybrid-casual games and the first one is My Little Planet!
